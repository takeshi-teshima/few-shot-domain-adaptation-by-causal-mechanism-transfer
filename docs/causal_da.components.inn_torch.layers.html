class ActNorm(dim, scale=1.0)¶

Bases: torch.nn.modules.module.Module

Activation Normalization. Initialize the bias and scale with a given mini batch so that the output per-channel have zero mean and unit variance for that. After initialization, bias and logs will be trained as parameters.

Parameters

  • dim (int) – dimension of the input.

  • scale (float) – a coefficient on the scaling parameter.
